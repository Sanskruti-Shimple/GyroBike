Our 3-D Project

The Reason:

So we from the FY decided to do some adventerous project and make something that would make everyones jaw drop, so we thought we should help our socity too. we pondered and got to the conclusion of helping handicapped teenagers/olders who never got a chance to ride a two wheeler bike. the knowledge we had about physics (thank you sir) we thought of building a bike that would stabalize itself and help to ride it without any disbalance.

The Procedure:

Our Groupwas on it. we were furious about the project, the desire was impecible. we distributed our work and starting working. the basic starting was of by the principle on which it should work. the theory and important concepts like rotaional motion, conservation of linear and angular momentum, torque, force needed, the torque to weight ratio. all were under our fingers. we implimented it practiacally and was succsessfully done with the stabalization parts. the website was also simultaneously been made and help us to get through this four wall, and get our project to be seen by others too. And also it helped to schowcase our project in the Covid situation.

Componenets and Technology used:

For the bike:

12V battery, high rpm DC motor, low rpm high torque DC motor, flywheel(disk), wheels, wooden parts-for body structure, connecting wires.

For the website:

HTML-5, CSS-3, JavaScript- for responsiveness

Principle:

conservation of angular mommentum, rate of change of angular momentum, gyroscopic torque due to spinning wheel.
